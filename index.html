What is Carbonite?

Understanding the cloud backup solution that protects your valuable data

Comprehensive Cloud Backup Solution

Carbonite is a leading cloud backup service designed to protect personal and business data against loss from hardware failure, ransomware, accidental deletion, and natural disasters. With automatic, continuous backup, Carbonite ensures your files are securely stored in the cloud and easily recoverable whenever you need them.

Founded in 2005, Carbonite has grown to become one of the most trusted names in cloud backup, serving millions of customers worldwide. The service offers various plans tailored to individual needs, from basic personal backup to comprehensive business solutions with advanced features.

Your Carbonite account serves as the central hub for managing your backup preferences, accessing files from anywhere, monitoring backup status, and restoring data when needed. Understanding how to properly access and navigate your account is essential for maximizing Carbonite's powerful data protection capabilities.

Key Features & Benefits

Discover what makes Carbonite an essential tool for data protection

Military-Grade Encryption

All files are encrypted with 128-bit or 256-bit Blowfish encryption before they leave your computer and remain encrypted while stored in Carbonite's secure data centers. This ensures your sensitive data remains protected at all times.

Automatic Continuous Backup

Once configured, Carbonite works silently in the background, automatically backing up new and changed files. There's no need to remember to run backups - your data is always protected.

Anywhere Access

Access your backed-up files from anywhere using Carbonite's web portal or mobile apps. View, download, and share files from your smartphone, tablet, or any computer with internet access.

Extended File Versioning

Carbonite retains previous versions of your files for weeks, months, or even years depending on your plan. This allows you to recover files as they existed at a specific point in time.

Courier Recovery Service

For major data loss events, Carbonite offers a courier recovery service where your data is shipped on an encrypted hard drive, saving significant time compared to downloading large amounts of data.

24/7 Customer Support

Carbonite provides award-winning customer support with experts available around the clock to assist with any questions or issues you may encounter with your backup service.

How to Login to Your Carbonite Account

Step-by-step instructions for accessing your account on different devices

Logging in on Desktop/Laptop (Windows/Mac)

Accessing your Carbonite account from a computer is straightforward:

  1. Open your preferred web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  2. Navigate to the official Carbonite login page
  3. Enter your Carbonite username (email address) in the first field
  4. Type your Carbonite password in the second field
  5. Check "Remember me" if you're using a personal device for quicker future access
  6. Click the "Sign In" button to access your account dashboard

Once logged in, you can manage your backup settings, view backup status, restore files, and access account management features.

Logging in on Mobile Devices (iOS/Android)

Carbonite offers dedicated mobile apps for convenient access:

  1. Download and install the Carbonite Mobile app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
  2. Launch the app on your mobile device
  3. Tap the "Sign In" button on the welcome screen
  4. Enter your Carbonite email address and password
  5. Enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint) if prompted for quicker future access
  6. Tap "Sign In" to access your files and backup status

The mobile app allows you to browse backed-up files, download important documents, view photos, and check backup status from anywhere.

Accessing via Web on Tablets

For tablet users, the web interface provides full functionality:

  1. Open the web browser on your tablet (Safari on iPad, Chrome on Android tablets)
  2. Visit the Carbonite login page
  3. Enter your login credentials (email and password)
  4. For easier input, use your tablet's password manager if available
  5. Tap "Sign In" to access your account
  6. Use the responsive interface to navigate your Carbonite dashboard

The web interface automatically adapts to your tablet's screen size, providing an optimized experience for managing your backups and files.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Common problems and solutions for Carbonite login challenges

I forgot my Carbonite password

If you've forgotten your Carbonite password, follow these steps to reset it:

  1. Go to the Carbonite login page
  2. Click on "Forgot password?" link below the sign-in button
  3. Enter the email address associated with your Carbonite account
  4. Check your email for a password reset message from Carbonite
  5. Click the password reset link in the email (link expires in 24 hours)
  6. Create a new strong password following the requirements (min 8 characters, upper/lower case, numbers)
  7. Log in with your new credentials

Note: If you don't receive the reset email, check your spam folder and ensure you're checking the email account associated with your Carbonite subscription.

My account is locked after multiple failed attempts

Carbonite automatically locks accounts after several unsuccessful login attempts to prevent unauthorized access:

  • Wait 30 minutes - Most temporary locks are automatically lifted after this period
  • Ensure Caps Lock is off and you're entering the correct email
  • Use the "Forgot Password" process to reset your credentials
  • If the issue persists after 30 minutes, contact Carbonite Support for assistance

To prevent future lockouts, consider using a password manager to store your credentials securely and ensure accurate entry.

I can't access my backup files after login

If you've successfully logged in but can't access your files:

  1. Check your subscription status - ensure your account is active and payments are current
  2. Verify backup status - look for any warnings or errors in your dashboard
  3. Confirm file selection - ensure the files you're looking for are selected for backup
  4. Check the "Deleted Files" section if you can't find specific documents
  5. Review file version history - you may need to restore an earlier version
  6. Try accessing from a different browser or device to isolate the issue

If files are missing due to accidental deletion on your computer, Carbonite retains deleted files for 30 days (longer on certain plans), so you can still restore them during this period.

I'm getting an "Invalid credentials" error

When receiving an invalid credentials error despite being sure of your password:

  • Check for typos in both email and password fields
  • Ensure Caps Lock isn't accidentally enabled
  • Try typing your password in a text editor to verify it before pasting
  • Clear browser cache and cookies, then attempt login again
  • Try logging in from a different browser or device
  • Reset your password following the "Forgot Password" procedure
  • If you recently changed your email, try logging in with your previous email address

If these steps don't resolve the issue, your account may have been compromised or deactivated - contact Carbonite Support for further assistance.
